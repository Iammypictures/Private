The meaning

The name carries
ancient instruction.

Tshivenda is one of South Africa's 11 official languages, spoken primarily in Limpopo. The closest known root — Tendani — carries one of the most audacious meanings a name can hold.

Three independent South African user submissions agreed: it means "Believe in God." A fourth translates it as "Agree as a statement or instruction." Not a passive noun. A command. A calling.

A child spoke this into an adult who had spent years studying belief, behaviour, and systems — without yet fully believing in himself. The timing was not accidental.
